The Four Forces of Flight

Every aircraft in flight experiences four primary forces: lift, weight, thrust, and drag. These forces work together to determine how an aircraft moves through the atmosphere.

Lift

Lift is the upward force generated by the wings that opposes the aircraft's weight.

Drag

Drag is the resistance created by air as the aircraft moves forward.
